Wire Fraud

Attackers intercept transaction instructions and redirect funds to fraudulent accounts.

Most common in agencies with large transaction volumes + limited training

📧

Invoice Fraud

Compromised email systems send fake invoices to clients, redirecting payments to attacker accounts.

Affects all agency sizes equally

🔑

Account Takeover

Attackers gain access to agent accounts and drain client escrow or falsify transactions.

Highest risk when staff use weak passwords & no MFA
