## **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

The Australian cellar door & DTC wine market represents a **$400-600 million annual opportunity** with significant untapped potential in customer data capture, email automation, and post-visit sales. WineNight can capture **5-10% of the cellar door market** ($20-60M) within 3-5 years by solving critical pain points: manual scorecards, low email capture (30% vs. 90% with tech), and zero follow-up infrastructure.

**Key finding:** Australian wineries are severely underinvesting in CRM and email automation—less than 20% use industry-specific CRM tools, creating a blue ocean opportunity.

## **6. MARKET VALIDATION SIGNALS (From 2018 Research)**

### Pain Points = Opportunity (High Confidence)

✅ **CRM adoption is LOW:**
- Only 13% use Customer Relationship Management systems
- Less than 20% use industry-specific CRM tools
- **Gap:** 80-87% of wineries are manually managing customer data

✅ **Email capture is WEAK:**
- Only ~1% of visitors join wine club
- Email capture not systematized (chargebacks for tastings ≠ email collection)
- **Gap:** Potential to 3-5x email capture with frictionless QR system

✅ **Manual tastings are STANDARD:**
- 49% of cellar doors offer formal seated tastings
- Nearly all use paper scorecards or manual tracking
- **Gap:** No one owns the blind tasting + scoring + recommendation workflow

✅ **Follow-up is NON-EXISTENT:**
- 60% of wineries have wine clubs, but recruitment is <1% of visitors
- No mention of post-visit email sequences or recommendations
- **Gap:** Huge untapped channel for repeat sales

✅ **Wine clubs show strong LTV:**
- Average member stays 3+ years
- 14% annual growth in club membership
- **Gap:** Club acquisition needs email + retention automation
