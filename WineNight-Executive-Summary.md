# WineNight: Executive Summary

**Australian Cellar Door Market Opportunity**

---

## BUSINESS OVERVIEW

WineNight is a blind-tasting scoring and customer automation platform purpose-built for Australian cellar doors. It captures customer data, generates personalized wine recommendations via email, and drives post-visit sales—solving the #1 pain point: manual scorecards and zero follow-up.

---

## MARKET OPPORTUNITY

### Market Size
- **Total Australian cellar door market:** $550M - $675M annually
- **Wineries with cellar doors:** 1,800 - 2,250
- **Annual cellar door visits:** 45-50 million
- **Target market (regional wineries, 5k-50k cases):** 400-500 wineries

### The Problem: Revenue Left on Table
**Typical cellar door: 25,000 visitors/year**

| Metric | Current | With WineNight | Improvement |
|--------|---------|-----------------|------------|
| Email capture | 30% (7,500) | 90% (22,500) | +15,000 emails |
| Post-visit conversion | 2% (150) | 12% (2,700) | +2,550 sales |
| Annual repeat revenue | $0 | $108,000 | +$108,000 |

**ROI: 81x** — Winery makes $81 for every $1 spent on subscription

---

## PRODUCT

### What We Build
1. **Winery login & dashboard** — Staff access, wine catalog, tasting management
2. **QR code tasting** — Customers scan, enter name, score wines (1-10 slider + flavor tags)
3. **Real-time staff dashboard** — Live results, wine leaderboard
4. **Email capture modal** — Automatic post-scoring prompt
5. **Personalized follow-up email** — Taste profile + 3-5 wine recommendations + shop links
6. **Basic analytics** — Top wines, customer preferences, email engagement

### MVP Timeline
- **Weeks 1-2:** Build winery features (auth, tasting, QR, email automation)
- **Weeks 3-4:** Beta with 5-10 Sydney wineries, gather testimonials
- **Weeks 5-8:** Cold outreach to 50+ regional wineries, close 5-10 paying customers
- **Weeks 9-12:** Launch personal tier (friend groups + $9.99 one-time)

**Development effort:** 45-50 hours (1.5 weeks full-time)

## MARKET VALIDATION

### Why This Opportunity is Real

✅ **CRM gap:** 87% of Australian wineries have NO industry CRM system  
✅ **Email capture failure:** Only 1% of cellar door visitors join wine club (massive untapped channel)  
✅ **Manual processes standard:** 49% still use paper scorecards or manual tally  
✅ **Zero competitors:** Vivino does reviews, CellarTracker is database — no one owns blind tasting + email automation workflow  
✅ **Strong wine club economics:** Members stay 3+ years, 14% annual growth (proves high lifetime value)  
✅ **Clear willingness to pay:** $99/month is 0.1% of typical winery's extra revenue from app = trivial cost
