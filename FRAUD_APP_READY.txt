🎯 THE 6 QUESTIONS

  1. Agency Size (1-5, 6-20, 20+ agents)
  2. Monthly Volume (<$100k, $100k-$500k, $500k+)
  3. Primary Focus (Rentals, Sales, Both)
  4. Fraud Training (None, Some, Robust)
  5. Fraud History (No, Yes/caught, Yes/lost)
  6. Email (Optional contact)

⚙️ SCORING ALGORITHM

  Wire Fraud:
    • Size (10-40) + Volume (5-35) + Training (5-30)
    • Targets high-value transactions via email compromise

  Invoice Fraud:
    • Volume (10-40) + Training (15-50)
    • Most common - impersonating vendors

  Account Takeover:
    • Training/Proficiency (20-65)
    • Unauthorized access to agency accounts

  Incident Multiplier: 1.0x → 1.2x → 1.4x
  Risk Levels: LOW (<35) | MEDIUM (35-59) | HIGH (≥60)
